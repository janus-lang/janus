# The Search for a Logo

## What is Janus
Janus is a systems language with precise incremental compilation and content-addressed builds.
Janus is an ambitious project that has the potential to change how we think about compilation and language design. It is built from the beginning with AI in mind and promises to deliver high-quality code without the AI hallucinating. Janus implements a deterministic incremental compilation system using cryptographic hashing to ensure correctness while minimizing rebuild work. The compiler tracks interface vs implementation changes separately, enabling optimal rebuild decisions.
Now we are looking for a logo and a mascot. (or both in one)

## Janus Logo Proposal

Janus is a name rich with symbolism—duality, gateways, and the transition between past and future. It deserves a strong, memorable identity that reflects its revolutionary doctrines. Drawing inspiration from Rust's approachable yet meaningful mascot, we've forged distinct logo proposals. Each concept is a different interpretation of the Janus philosophy.

---

## Abstract Logo Proposal (from AI): The Abstract Symbol (The Dual Arrow)

**Concept:** A clean, geometric, and instantly recognizable symbol built from two arrows forming a "J". It represents the core technical premise: the duality between Interface and Implementation, or the transition from Source to Binary.

**Description:** Two opposing arrows are fused together into a single, cohesive shape. The negative space between them also forms a checkmark, symbolizing correctness and verification. It's highly scalable and looks excellent on documentation, app icons, and CLI tools.

**Why it works:** It's professional, modern, and embodies the language's architectural philosophy in a single, minimal mark. It appeals to engineers who appreciate clean, conceptual design.

---

## Mascot Logo Proposal (from Markus): The Mascot (Janus - The Clever Lynx)

**Concept:** A modern, approachable mascot inspired by the two-faced Janus god, but reimagined as a clever, forward/backward-looking lynx. The lynx symbolizes sharp vision, intelligence, and perception—perfect for a language focused on precision and debugging.

**Description:** The lynx faces forward, but its head has a second set of "eye spots" on the back of its ears, a common trait in real lynxes, cleverly representing the two-faced nature. One eye is looking at source code on a screen, the other is looking at perfect, compiled binary output. It's sleek, intelligent, and friendly.

**Why it works:** It's memorable, ownable, and builds brand character like Rust's Ferris the Crab. It communicates intelligence and dual perspective without being literal or intimidating.

---

## The Two to Ship

1. **Logo Proposal 1 — Abstract Dual Arrow (primary mark)** Clean, scalable, timeless. It encodes Interface↔Implementation and "verified" in one glyph, works at favicon size, and looks pro on slides, crates, and the CLI.

2. **Logo Proposal 2 — Janus Lynx (community mascot)** Unique and ownable. The lynx ear "eye-spots" sell the two-faced idea without being literal. Great for docs, swag, and tutorials while the Dual Arrow remains the official mark.

## Implementation Notes

### Primary Mark Usage
- **Favicon**: Dual Arrow at 16x16, 32x32, 64x64
- **CLI Tools**: ASCII art version for terminal output
- **Documentation**: SVG format for crisp scaling
- **App Icons**: Dual Arrow with subtle gradient

### Mascot Usage
- **Community Materials**: Tutorials, blog posts, conference swag
- **Documentation Headers**: Friendly face for getting started guides
- **Error Messages**: Helpful lynx providing debugging hints
- **Social Media**: Memorable character for brand recognition

### Brand Guidelines
- **Never modify the core geometry** of the Dual Arrow
- **Maintain minimum clear space** around both marks
- **Use approved color palettes** or monochrome versions only
- **Lynx personality**: Intelligent, helpful, never condescending

## Technical Specifications

### Dual Arrow Symbol
- **Minimum size**: 16px (favicon)
- **Optimal sizes**: 32px, 64px, 128px, 256px
- **File formats**: SVG (primary), PNG (fallback), ICO (favicon)
- **Stroke width**: 2px at base size, scales proportionally

### Janus Lynx Mascot
- **Minimum size**: 48px (maintains ear detail visibility)
- **Optimal sizes**: 128px, 256px, 512px
- **File formats**: SVG (primary), PNG with transparency
- **Style**: Clean vector art, minimal detail for scalability
